New to the Grand Line Atlas?

This is a fan-made, unofficial map of the One Piece world. Drag the globe to spin it, scroll to zoom, and click any dot to open that island's log: what happened there, who was involved, and where the story goes next.

What the dots mean

Explored: the crew has already been here
Current: this is where the story stands right now
Upcoming: named, but not yet reached
Memorial: a moment, not a place to visit

The Log Pose dial

The needle up top always points at whichever island you last selected, a nod to the in-story compass that locks onto the next island instead of north.

Everything else on this site

🕸 Graph: browse every crew's and organization's (Marines, CP9, the World Government…) relationships without clicking into an island first. Any character's own profile also cross-links out to the Family Tree, Story Graph, Origin Tree, and Bounty Chart when they have data there.
🌐 Full Story Graph: every character, crew, place, fruit, and ship in one connected map, with a built-in path finder between any two.
⏳ Time Travel: drag through the sagas and watch the world (and the crew's ship) unlock arc by arc.
💵 Bounty Board: every wanted pirate, ranked, searchable, grouped by crew and by whether they're alive, deceased, or unconfirmed.
⛵ Route Animation: watch the ship actually sail the whole journey so far, stop by stop.
❓ Ask the Atlas: type a plain-English question about anyone or anything in the story.
📖 Glossary / 🧭 Quiz: world-building terms explained once, and a quick trivia check.
