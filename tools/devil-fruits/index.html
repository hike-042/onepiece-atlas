How Devil Fruits work

ParameciaThe broadest category: a single superhuman trait or ability that covers everything short of transforming into an animal or an element.
ZoanGrants transformation into a specific animal, plus a human-animal hybrid mid-form. Ancient Zoans (extinct species) and Mythical Zoans (legendary creatures) are progressively rarer and stronger.
LogiaThe rarest class: lets the user become, generate, and control a natural element outright, with near-total physical immunity while transformed.
  • Only one person can hold a given fruit's power at a time; when its user dies, that power resurfaces in a fresh fruit somewhere else in the world.
  • Eating a second Devil Fruit is fatal to nearly everyone who's tried it; Marshall D. Teach is the sole known exception, and even that isn't explained in-story.
  • Every user permanently loses the ability to swim, and can be weakened the same way by Seastone, a mineral that saps their strength on contact.
  • With enough mastery, a power can rarely reach Awakening, letting its effects extend beyond the user's own body.
